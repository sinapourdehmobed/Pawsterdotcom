About
We are Animal Lovers.
What we do:

Pawster is one of the most unique ideas of social networking among people and their pets. The inspiration behind Pawster is to develop a social network among people’s pets. The motivation of this project is derived by the need to uphold the social healthcare of pets/animals. Social anxiety is a real issue among animals, especially dogs who are highly social animals. 39 percent of dogs have no other animal to keep them company while their owners are out. Besides social needs pets also need exercise/waks to avoid health problems. 63 percent of dogs do not get walked daily and 62 percent of dogs didn’t attend socialization classes as a puppy. Up to 40 per cent of dogs are overweight. Obesity can cause major health problems such as diabetes and reduce quality of life and shorten lifespan.
